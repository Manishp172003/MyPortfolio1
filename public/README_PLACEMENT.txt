---------------------------------------------------------
MANISH PORTFOLIO - VIDEO BACKGROUND DEPLOYMENT BLUEPRINT
---------------------------------------------------------

To activate your premium cinematic loop in the background of your Hero section:

1. Locate your video file (the cinematic video of Manish with deep backlighting).
2. Rename that video file to exactly:
   video.mp4

3. Place it directly inside this "/public" folder:
   /public/video.mp4

4. Deploy or start your local server. The Vite bundler will map this file to "/" route.
5. The application will immediately discover the file, auto-play it in a continuous loop, and keep it properly muted in the background.

Ensure your video uses standard MP4 H.264 format for ultimate, lag-free cross-browser compatibility!
